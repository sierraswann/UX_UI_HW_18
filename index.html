Hey Ya'll! I'm Sierra; an inquisitive mind, creative soul, and sweet Georgia peach. As a Theater major in college, I have utilized my listening, collaboration, and creative skills to help me on my journey as a UX/UI designer. My goal is to continue to create designs that are not only beautiful, but also solve problems and make technology accessible to everyone.

Case Studies

The Wander Woman App

This app was built for women who travel by women who travel.

The Goal: To give women a safety net while they explore without sacrificing their need for rich cultural experiences.

U.S. Department of Labor Homepage Re-Design

This re-design is of the U.S. Department of Labor's homepage. It needed a major revamping.

The Goal: To make the U.S. Department of Labor's website more accessible to the average American.

The Atlanta Beagle Rescue

The Atlanta Beagle Rescue needed a website that properly reflects the successful work they do in saving and re-homing beagles.

The Goal: To give the Atlanta Beagle Rescue a professional landing page that offers credibility to their work and solidifies them as a thought leader on Beagles in Atlanta and its surrounding areas.
